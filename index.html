About Me

Get to know the person behind the designs

Hi again, here's a little more about me!

Along with being a designer and video editor, I am a Global Business and Digital Arts student @ University of Waterloo! My entire life, I have valued any time I could get to flex my creative and problem-solving muscles. Since coming to university, I have gotten the opportunity to spend time growing these muscles and have developed a passion for creating works that immediately pop out and create an engaging experience for anyone who stumbles upon them.

I love exploring new experiences, whether that be learning new skills, watching new TV shows or movies, tasting new foods, or having new adventures with friends. I believe that life is too short not to explore any experiences that one is interested in and is in their reach.
